<p>CADENCE <strong>Business Intelligence and Development</strong> unit is a
dedicated business team for active industry engagements. The BIDU is a
joint partnership between the Biomedical Sciences Industry Partnership
Office (BMSIPO) and the CADENCE Programme Office.</p>
<p>The platform is responsible for developing outreach strategies, as well
as developing and sustaining strategic academic and commercial partnerships
to drive translational cardiovascular research in Singapore.</p>
<p>Through this platform, CADENCE aims to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Serve as a single point of contact to engage industry partners to identify
and pursue collaborative opportunities that leverage access to the resources
and research capabilities of the CADENCE Joint Platforms</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide a business intelligence perspective to CADENCE's Joint Platforms
in order to enhance the clinical and commercial impact of our research</p>
</li>
</ul>
